Wacky Twos

Written and recorded by me over the last ~14-16 years or so.

The album art is another math puzzle that brings together the idea of 2/3 and plays off of themes of growth and development. Hidden within the twisty turns are secret codes that encode codes, themselves codes. The information required to solve the puzzle are all contatined within the space of the art. Hint: Primes.

If you believe you have a solution, or want to chat about music, or talk about how cool math can be, contact: jnoughte@gmail.com
